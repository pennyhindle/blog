I'm Penny, a recent Computer Science graduate based in Leeds, currently working as a Software Developer at X-Lab. I am also a mentor, public speaker, teacher and influencer.

Career

Started studying Computer Science with Industrial Placement at the University of Leeds.

Completed a 3 month internship as a Frontend Developer at Connectably. I then went on to do a placement year at X-Lab as a Technical Project Manager. I started teaching with Code First: Girls and became a STEM ambassador.

In 2018, I finished my Placement year and went back to university. I started working at Leeds City College running a workshop to teach girls how to code in C++. Featured on 1MWIS

In 2019, I Graduated from the University of Leeds with a 2:1 with an award award for "The student who does the most to foster a good interaction between the School of Computing and other communities." I re-joined X-Lab as a sofrware developer. I was nominated for a Northern Power Women 'One to Watch' award and shortlisted for the DevelopHER 'Best Newcomer' award. I am one of the winners of the 2019 #TechWomen100 awards.

This year, I have started to build on my public speaking skills. I have also started on an exciting startup adventure alongside work.
