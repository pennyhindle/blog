I'm Penny, a recent Computer Science graduate based in Leeds, currently working as a Software Developer.

Career

In 2015 I started studying Computer Science with Industrial Placement at the University of Leeds

In 2017, I completed a 3 month intersnhip as a Frontend Developer at Connectably. I then went on to do a placement year at X-Lab as a Technical Project Manager. I started teaching with Code First: Girls and became a STEM ambassador.

In 2018, I finished my Placement year and went back to university. I started working at Leeds City College running a workshop to teach girls how to code in C++.

This year, I Graduated from the university of Leeds with a 2:1 and re-joined X-Lab as a sofrware developer. I was nominated for a Northern Power Women 'One to Watch' award and shortlisted for the DevelopHER 'Best Newcomer' award. I am one of the winners of the 2019 #TechWomen100 awards.
